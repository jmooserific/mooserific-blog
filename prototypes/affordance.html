Affordance signal

Backlog #4 — umber marks both static meta you read and chrome you click, with no cue between them on touch. This prototype keeps the single-umber system and adds non-color signals: a resting accent/15 hairline on icon controls, and underline-on-interactive for text controls. Toggle touch mode to judge the resting state with hover removed.
